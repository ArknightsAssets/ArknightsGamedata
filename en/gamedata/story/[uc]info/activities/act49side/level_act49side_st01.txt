After the Feast of a Hundred Delicacies, strange phenomena manifest around Yan. The True Lung makes an agreement with the Grand Commandant and sets the day of the full moon as the deadline before which Yan will send troops to quell the Sui. Zuo Le leaves the Garden of Grotesqueries, hurrying back to Baizao with a token in hand. At the same time, Ch'en enters a dream woven by the Feranmuts.
